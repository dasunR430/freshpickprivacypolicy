FreshPick Account Deletion

At FreshPick, we value your privacy and provide this guide to help you delete your account and associated data.

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the FreshPick app on your device.
  2. Navigate to the Settings menu.
  3. Select "Account Management".
  4. Choose "Delete Account".
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm your decision.
  6. You will receive a confirmation email within 24 hours.
  7. Click the confirmation link in the email to finalize the deletion process.

Data Deletion Information

When you request account deletion, we will:

  • Permanently delete your user profile
  • Remove all personal information associated with your account
  • Delete your order history, saved preferences, and shopping lists

Note: The deletion process may take up to 30 days to complete. Some information may be retained for legal or financial record-keeping purposes for up to 90 days after deletion.

If you have any questions or need assistance with account deletion, please contact our support team at support@freshpick.com.
