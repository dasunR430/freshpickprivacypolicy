YourAppName Account Deletion

We value your privacy and provide this guide to help you delete your account and associated data from YourAppName.

Steps to Request Account Deletion

  1. Open the YourAppName app on your device.
  2. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm your decision.
  4. You will receive a confirmation email within 24 hours.
  5. Click the confirmation link in the email to finalize the deletion process.

Data Deletion Information

When you request account deletion, we will:

  • Permanently delete your user profile
  • Remove all personal information associated with your account
  • Delete your saved preferences and activity history

Note: The deletion process may take up to 30 days to complete. Some information may be retained for legal or security purposes for an additional 90 days.

If you have any questions, please contact our support team at support@yourappname.com.
